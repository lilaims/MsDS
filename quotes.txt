Science is not about sophisticated instrumentation, maths or theories: it
is about evaluating our knowledge.

Knowledge can be represented as a
Proposition (statement, law)
Smaller animals have a faster heartrate.
Narrative (description, story)
The size of an animal seems to be related to its heart rate. In
general, larger animals tend to have a slow heart rate. For instance,
the humpback whale...
Model (mathematical or computer)
r = 235 × m−1/4
, where r is the heart rate and m is the body mass.
